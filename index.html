If you're like me, you are a beer snob. Also, if you're like me, you have certain stores you go to for certain beer. Some brands are cheaper at some stores, more expensive in others. Often store X will carry beer A cheaper than store Y but store Y will carry beer B cheaper than at store X. BeerCheer is aimed at helping you find the beer you want at the best known local prices.

Now, there is often a tradeoff to the distance you will have to travel to get a good price on your favorite beverage and BeerCheer also aims to help you make that tradeoff. Given an estimated cost of your time, the approximate price of gas, and the beer price, BeerCheer will try to let you know how much you are saving by driving that extra 5 miles to get 80 cents off of that great locally brewed IPA.
